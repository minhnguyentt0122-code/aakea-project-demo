### F-08.2 Data Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)

**Summary**

Customers request deletion of their personal data. The system processes erasure within TC-34: 30-day legally required window, with exemptions for financial records required by law (TC-32: 7 years).

**Business Rules**

| BR # | Rule |
|---|---|
| BR-01 | TC-34: Erasure completed within 30 days of verified request. |
| BR-02 | Financial records (orders, invoices, tax records) retained for TC-32: 7 years per legal requirement — PII fields anonymised, financial totals retained. |
| BR-03 | Erasure covers: name, email, phone, addresses, project wishlists, reviews (anonymised), session tokens. |
| BR-04 | Account closure (F-01.6) triggers erasure request automatically. |
| BR-05 | Erasure request confirmed by re-entering password. Email confirmation sent before processing begins. |

**Permission Matrix**

| Method | Endpoint | Description | Guest | Customer | Trade | Ops | Content | Super Admin |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **POST** | /api/v1/account/erasure-request | Submit erasure request | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ |
| **GET** | /api/v1/admin/erasure-requests | List erasure requests | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| **PATCH** | /api/v1/admin/erasure-requests/:requestId | Process erasure | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |

**API Contract**

| Method | Endpoint | Description | Request Body / Params | Response |
|---|---|---|---|---|

**TC References:** TC-34 (30-day completion), TC-32 (7-year financial retention), TC-35 (erasure audit log)
