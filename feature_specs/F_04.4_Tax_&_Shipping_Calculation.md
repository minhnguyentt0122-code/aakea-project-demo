## M04 — Checkout & Payment

---

### F-04.4 Tax & Shipping Calculation

**Summary**

Calculates US sales tax (Stripe Tax/TaxJar) and EU VAT breakdown at checkout, and computes shipping cost based on destination zone, product weight, and delivery method. Trade accounts with valid VAT registration receive VAT-exempt checkout.

**Background / Context**

Premium furniture attracts high-value orders where a surprise tax charge at checkout is especially damaging to conversion. US and EU tax models are fundamentally different — US adds tax on top, EU includes VAT in the displayed price. Trade accounts in the EU may be eligible for VAT exemption under the B2B reverse charge mechanism, requiring different invoice generation.

**User Story**

As a customer at checkout, I want to see the exact total including tax and shipping before paying so there are no surprise charges.

**Flow — Happy Path**

1. Customer selects delivery address and method.
2. System calculates shipping: zone lookup + weight-based rate + white-glove surcharge if applicable.
3. Stripe Tax API called with delivery address and product line items.
4. US: sales tax calculated by state/county and added as line item. EU: VAT breakdown shown (included in price). Trade EU VAT-exempt: VAT line shows €0 with 'VAT exempt — reverse charge' label.
5. Order summary updated: items subtotal, trade discount (if applicable), shipping, tax, total.
6. All amounts locked. Final total passed to PaymentIntent.

**Flow — Edge Cases**

| Edge Case | Expected System Behaviour |
|---|---|
| Tax API call times out | Fallback: use admin-configured flat tax rate for destination state/country. Log for reconciliation. |
| Free shipping threshold met | Shipping line item shows '$0.00 — Free Shipping (orders over $[threshold])'. |
| EU trade VAT-exempt customer | VAT line shows '€0.00 — VAT exempt (B2B reverse charge)'. VAT-exempt invoice generated on order confirmation. |
| Trade customer purchasing in USD (US) | Standard US sales tax applies — VAT exemption only applies to EU VAT, not US sales tax. |

**Business Rules**

| BR # | Rule |
|---|---|
| BR-01 | Tax calculation is authoritative at PaymentIntent creation. Pre-confirmation summary is an estimate. |
| BR-02 | EU VAT included in displayed prices (not added at checkout). Shown as breakdown: 'Includes [rate]% VAT: €[amount]'. |
| BR-03 | EU trade accounts with verified VAT registration (OD-06): VAT-exempt checkout. Invoice generated with customer VAT number and 'Reverse Charge' notation. |
| BR-04 | US sales tax added at checkout as additional line item. Nexus states per CR-10. |
| BR-05 | Shipping rate = `flat_rate + (weight_kg × per_kg_rate)` from `shipping_rates` table by zone. White-glove always has surcharge. |
