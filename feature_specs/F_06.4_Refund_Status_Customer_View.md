## M06 — Returns & Refunds

---

### F-06.4 Refund Status (Customer View)

**Summary**

Customers can check refund status from their order page without contacting support, including estimated credit date based on TC-23.

**Permission Matrix**

| Method | Endpoint | Description | Guest | Customer | Trade | Ops | Content | Super Admin |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **GET** | /api/v1/orders/:orderId/refund | Get refund status | ❌ | ⚠ | ⚠ | ✅ | ❌ | ✅ |

*⚠ Own orders only.*

**API Contract**

| Method | Endpoint | Description | Request Body / Params | Response |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **GET** | /api/v1/orders/:orderId/refund | Refund status | — | `200: { refundId, amount, refundType, status, estimatedCreditDate, stripeRefundId }` |

**TC References:** TC-23 (≤12 business day total resolution)

**Acceptance Criteria**

| # | Criteria | Test Type | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| AC-01 | GET shows estimated credit date based on TC-23 window. | Unit | — |
| AC-02 | Customer cannot view another customer's refund status — returns 403. | Security | — |
