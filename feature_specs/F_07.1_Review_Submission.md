## M07 — Reviews & Notifications

---

### F-07.1 Review Submission

**Summary**

Verified purchasers submit star ratings and text reviews for products they have received. One review per customer per product. Reviews are critical social proof for premium design purchases.

**User Story**

As a customer who received a product, I want to leave a rating and review so that other design buyers can benefit from my experience with the quality and accuracy of the piece.

**Flow — Happy Path**

1. TC-24: 7 days after delivery, customer receives review prompt email (E-06).
2. Customer visits product page or `/account/orders/:orderId` — 'Write a Review' shown for delivered items within TC-25: 180-day window.
3. Customer selects star rating (1–5), writes review title and body, submits.
4. `POST /reviews` creates review with `status='pending'` (awaiting moderation, F-07.2).
5. Customer sees 'Thank you for your review! It will appear once approved.'

**Business Rules**

| BR # | Rule |
|---|---|
| BR-01 | TC-24: Only customers with a delivered order containing the product may submit a review. Verified by purchase check on server. |
| BR-02 | One review per customer per product. Duplicate returns 422 with link to edit existing review. |
| BR-03 | Reviews must be approved by admin moderation (F-07.2) before appearing on product page. |
| BR-04 | TC-25: Review submission window: 180 days from delivery. 'Write a Review' hidden after 180 days. |

**Input Validation Rules**

| Field | Type | Required | Validation Rules | Error Message Shown |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| productId | UUID | Yes | Must be a product the customer received in a delivered order. | 403: 'You can only review products you have purchased.' |
| rating | Integer | Yes | 1–5 inclusive. | 'Please select a rating between 1 and 5 stars' |
| title | String | No | Max 150 chars. Plain text. | 'Review title must be 150 characters or fewer' |
| body | String | Yes | Min 10 chars, max 2000 chars. Plain text. | 'Review must be between 10 and 2000 characters' |

**Permission Matrix**

| Method | Endpoint | Description | Guest | Customer | Trade | Ops | Content | Super Admin |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **POST** | /api/v1/reviews | Submit review | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| **GET** | /api/v1/reviews/me | Get own reviews | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |
| **PATCH** | /api/v1/reviews/:reviewId | Edit own review | ❌ | ⚠ | ⚠ | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |

*⚠ Own reviews only.*

**API Contract**

| Method | Endpoint | Description | Request Body / Params | Response |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **POST** | /api/v1/reviews | Submit review | `{ productId, rating, title?, body }` | `201: { reviewId, status: 'pending' }` / `403` / `422` |
| **PATCH** | /api/v1/reviews/:reviewId | Edit review | `{ rating?, title?, body? }` | `200: { review }` — resets status to 'pending' |

**TC References:** TC-24 (verified purchase + 7-day prompt delay), TC-25 (180-day submission window), TC-26 (48-hr moderation SLA)
