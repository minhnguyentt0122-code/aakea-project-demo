### F-08.3 Data Retention Jobs

**Summary**

Automated background jobs enforce data retention policies: purging unverified accounts, anonymising old orders, archiving audit logs, and processing trade account data in line with GDPR.

**Business Rules**

| BR # | Rule |
|---|---|
| BR-01 | TC-31: Unverified accounts inactive for >30 days are soft-deleted by the nightly retention job. |
| BR-02 | TC-32: Orders older than 7 years are pseudonymised (customer PII replaced with hashed identifiers). Financial totals, configuration details, and line items retained. |
| BR-03 | TC-33: Server/application logs older than 90 days auto-purged. |
| BR-04 | TC-35: Audit logs older than 3 years archived to cold storage (S3 Glacier). |
| BR-05 | All retention job runs logged with: job name, run timestamp, records affected, errors. |
| BR-06 | Trade account application data retained for 5 years from application date per financial compliance requirements (Legal to confirm). |

**Permission Matrix**
