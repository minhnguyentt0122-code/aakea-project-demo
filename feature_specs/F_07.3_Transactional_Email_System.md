### F-07.3 Transactional Email System

**Summary**

All system-triggered emails are dispatched via SendGrid/SES through BullMQ queues with TC-29 retry logic. Email templates are managed in the admin panel without deployment. Brand voice and visual design must reflect HomeStyle's premium positioning.

**Business Rules**

| BR # | Rule |
|---|---|
| BR-01 | TC-29: Email retry: 3× at 1-minute intervals, then 1 final attempt at 15 minutes. After 4 failures: dead-letter queue. Admin alerted. |
| BR-02 | All emails rendered from versioned templates stored in DB. Template changes do not require deployment. |
| BR-03 | Unsubscribe link in all marketing-adjacent emails per CAN-SPAM / GDPR. Transactional emails (order confirm, dispatch, refund) include account settings link. |
| BR-04 | Email templates must reflect premium brand aesthetic — high-resolution product imagery, clean typography. |
